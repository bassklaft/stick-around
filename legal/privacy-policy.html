FloofLife — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2026

FloofLife ("the App") is operated by Maxwell Klafter ("we," "us," or "our"), based in Brooklyn, New York, USA.

This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information.

Summary

FloofLife stores all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on our servers. We do not have servers that hold your data.

What information the App handles

When you use FloofLife, you may provide:

  • Your pet's name, species, breed, age, weight, and photo
  • Notes and observations you log about your pet
  • Your interactions with checklists and other features

All of this information is stored exclusively on your device using your phone's local storage (SQLite and AsyncStorage). It is not transmitted to us or any third party.

What we do NOT do

  • We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or contact information
  • We do not collect your location and store it. The App may request location permission to display nearby emergency veterinarians and current weather conditions, but this information is used in real time and is not retained or transmitted.
  • We do not use analytics services that track your behavior
  • We do not use third-party advertising networks
  • We do not sell, rent, or share any data
  • We do not have user accounts because we do not store data centrally

Photos and camera

The App may request access to your photo library or camera so you can add a photo of your pet to their profile. Photos remain on your device and are never uploaded.

Children

FloofLife is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

If we change how the App handles data in future versions, we will update this policy and disclose changes prominently within the App.
